My name is Konrad Paszkiewicz, paskon is my internet nickname.
I am digital product designer living in Kraków, Poland.

Origin story

Since I remember — I've had a huge fascination with technology. As a teenager, I was constantly browsing forums, reading cracked articles, watching too many tutorials and quoting the latest memes. That’s how I became internet savver.

My career started as a software developer but it soon became clear that my true passion was design — now, combining both skillsets. I’m currently working at Capgemini executing web and mobile design projects for the company and its clients from all over the world. Working end-to-end from concept creation through to delivery. Lead projects through their complete lifespan from planning, concept development, prototyping, wireframing and design through implementation and post launch evaluation.

Skills

By combining intersection of user experience and human-centered machine learning knowledge, I help clients design for the future while simultaneously evolving their existing businesses. My work focuses on three foundational areas of client success. The highest standards apply at every level.
‍
I shaping personalized user experiences to help expand the definition of predictive experiences. I am comfortable in uncharted territories where design hasn’t historically found itself or where the rules and engagements aren’t already established.
